# The Chamber's Zero Point

---

In the reign of Truth each thing in creation must reflect or express the inherent truth-seed of itself. The example closest to this state is given to us in the Mother's chamber, where each centimetre of its space form, as well as each object or element of the Room's design, reveals the dharma of the structure. The truth of its inner being is made evident to all who have new eyes to see in a new way, and the object itself bears no self-generated veils. The veils that may hide the chamber's light belong only to the aspirant who has not partaken of the new vision, and not to the Room itself. In the Reign of Truth each form will thus be a perfect representation of the seed of Truth that was its beginning; at no time in the process of its development from infancy to maturity will any perverting influence interfere to distort the form that arises from the core-seed. Individuality and personality will be a perfect harmonisation of two complementary poles of being—of Sun and Moon; essence and form will be a balanced expression in Matter of two dimensions of universal being.

During the period of transition to this total reign of Truth, it is helpful for the initiate to be given a means whereby he or she can have a direct perception of the seed of an object or element of Truth, and also its outer form which is a perfect expression of that inner bija. The world today, in this transitional stage, knows no such form except the chamber of the Divine Mother.

In our universe a simple way of coming to understand the true essence of being is by finding an element's place within a higher vision of Time, for this is the dimension in which the growth process from seed to full form is accomplished. It is also in this very dimension that a falsification can occur and the truth unfolding become distorted. Today this occurs because we have lost sight of the inner seed-essence of all created things, due to the fact that we have been conditioned into believing that falsehood is an intrinsic element of the Manifestation and that in the Absolute alone, beyond creation, can Reality be experienced. The human being today fails to see the seed-essence that is the bija of that very Timeless Absolute, as it exists after the transmutation it undergoes in order to begin the process of evolution from the involved seed. In this primordial state there is no question of Falsehood and Ignorance; these arise at a later stage of development and are the veils that gradually accumulate around…

## Key Concepts

- **Everything begins as a "truth-seed" (bija)** — an inner essence that precedes form.
- **A perfect form expresses that inner seed without distortion** — no veils between essence and manifestation.
- **The Mother's chamber** is presented as the closest existing example of form perfectly reflecting essence — every centimetre reveals the dharma of the structure.
- **Falsehood and ignorance are not original** — they arise later as distortions during development, not as intrinsic properties of creation.
- **Time is the dimension where seed becomes form** — and also where falsification can occur.
- **The "zero point"** is that primordial state before distortion, where the truth-seed exists in its pure transmuted form.

**In short:** True form = the visible expression of an invisible seed of truth. Distortion is acquired, not inherent.
