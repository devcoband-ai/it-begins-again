# Human Design System

**Creator:** Ra Uru Hu (born Robert Alan Krakower), 1987
**Origin:** Eight-day mystical experience on Ibiza; spent 25 years developing and teaching until his death in 2011

---

## What It Is

A synthesis system that integrates:
- **I Ching** — 64 hexagrams mapped to 64 gates
- **Kabbalah** — Tree of Life translated into a channel system
- **Hindu-Brahmin chakra system** — expanded from 7 to 9 energy centers
- **Astrology** — planetary positions at birth
- **Quantum physics / genetics** — 64 codons mapped to 64 hexagrams

## The Bodygraph

A chart generated from birth data. Nine energy centers connected by 36 channels and 64 gates.

- **Defined centers** — consistent, reliable, generating energy (colored in)
- **Open/undefined centers** — receptive, absorbing, conditioned by environment (white)
