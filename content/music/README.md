# Music

Everything music — lyrics, recordings, voice memos, Suno tracks, chord progressions, song concepts, production notes.

## Where Things Go

| What you have | Where it goes |
|---|---|
| Finished or in-progress tracks | Right here in `music/` |
| Rough ideas — a melody, a lyric fragment, a voice memo, a vibe | `music/ideas/` |

## What Counts as a Music Idea

Anything. A line you can't get out of your head. A chord change you heard somewhere. A voice memo from a drive. A genre mashup that shouldn't work but might. A song title with no song yet.

Drop it in `music/ideas/`. It doesn't need to be a song. It just needs to be a start.

## File Tips

- **Voice memos:** MP3, M4A, WAV — whatever your phone records in. Don't convert, just upload.
- **Lyrics:** Plain text or markdown. Name it something you'll recognize later.
- **Suno tracks:** Export and drop them here. Include the prompt you used if you remember it — helps with iteration.
- **Naming:** `slow-burn-country-sketch.mp3` beats `recording-47.m4a`. But don't let naming stop you from uploading.
