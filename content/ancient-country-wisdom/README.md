# Ancient Country Wisdom

David Perry's creative archive — art, writing, music, and 20 years of analog press work.

## What Goes Where

- **art/** — visual art, illustrations, photography, scans of original work
- **writing/** — stories, drafts, notes, book material
- **music/** — recordings, lyrics, song notes
- **press/** — published pieces, prints, analog press work, scans

## How This Works

David sends material to Jeff. Jeff gets it into the repo. Nothing gets published without David's say-so — this is a workspace, not a stage.

Everything here belongs to David.
