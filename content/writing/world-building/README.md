# World Building — It Begins, Again

This folder contains the raw material of the world the story lives in. Not outlines. Not plot notes. The *place* — its geography, its people, its sounds, its rules.

## Files

| File | What It Covers |
|------|---------------|
| `milledgeville.md` | The town itself — timeline, geography, real addresses, DoDo, the strip |
| `venue-arcs.md` | Five venues, five arcs — how each enters and deepens through the narrative |
| `characters.md` | Every named person in the story — who they are, where they appear, what's known |
| `timeline.md` | Chronology — 1989 to present day, what happened when |
| `sensory-details.md` | What the places look, sound, smell, taste, and feel like |
| `literary-dna.md` | The narrative influences — not cited in the text, but shaping the prose |
| `threads.md` | Recurring motifs, images, and ideas that connect across chapters |

## Rules

- Nothing in this folder appears in the book verbatim. This is scaffolding, not prose.
- Questions are as valuable as answers. Leave blanks where memory hasn't filled in yet.
- Real addresses, real names, real dates when possible. This is memoir, not fiction.
- Update after every writing session. The world-building is a living document.
