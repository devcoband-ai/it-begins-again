# Timeline

Chronology of events — real dates when known, approximate when not.

---

## 1988
- Jeff graduates high school in Marietta, GA

## 1988–1989 — The Lost Year
- Something happens. Details withheld from the narrative.
- Result: Jeff arrives at college a year late, age 19, starting over

## Summer 1989
- Jeff arrives at Milledgeville for summer session at Georgia College
- Knows absolutely nobody
- Finds DoDo's Pool Hall (128 W Hancock) — his first place in town
- Meets Roger behind the bar
- Learns about the Opera House (124 W Hancock) from a girl in his first 8am class
- **The strip does not yet exist.** Only DoDo's pool hall + Opera House. Cameron's, The Brick, Brewers — none built yet.

## ~1990–1993 — The Strip Gets Built
- Scott Cameron opens Cameron's (north side of Hancock)
- Mitch Brooks opens The Brick (136 W Hancock) — 1993 confirmed
- Someone opens Brewers (address unknown)
- Jeff's generation builds the physical infrastructure of the scene

## 1993
- Jeff is playing in a cover band at a fraternity party
- David Perry's band **Hero** opens — all original, art-house funk-metal fusion
- Jeff sees David play for the first time at Cameron's (Ch. 1)

## ~1993–1994
- Jeff meets Rich at a battle of the bands
- Jeff runs into Rich again at Brewers (a coffee shop)
- They talk about Bob Dylan
- Jeff is working in the kitchen at The Brick, enrolled as a grad student, drifting
- His band has dissolved

## ~1994
- Rich tells Jeff that he and David are forming **Ancient Country Wisdom** with others
- Jeff drives to Sandersville for first rehearsal
- Meets Noah and Greg
- Locks in with Greg's drumming — finds the pocket
- David's voice enters. Everything clicks.
- ACW begins.

## ~1994–late 1990s
- ACW active period
- _Details TBD — the bulk of the band's story lives here_

## Late 1990s–2000s
- Band ends (how? when? — not yet described)
- Members scatter
- Jeff moves into technology career

## ~2003–2008
- DoDo (James E. Hollis) wins the Georgia Lottery

## ~2010
- Jeff and David lose contact (last spoke ~2010 per Ch. 2)

## ~2016
- DoDo dies
- Pool hall left to his fiancée

## April 2017
- DoDo's Pool Hall sold to Frank Pendergast (Union-Recorder article)
- Pendergast also owns The Brick — consolidation of the strip

## ~2024–2025
- David calls Jeff after fifteen years of silence (Ch. 2)
- Cloud seeding conversation (Ch. 3)
- Human Design / bodygraph (Ch. 4)

## 2025
- Hancock County man wins $1M lottery at Texaco on N. Columbia St, Milledgeville

## 2026
- Jeff makes "Time Is Thin" on Suno. Sends to David. No response. (Ch. 6)
- Jeff sees social media photo posted by mutual friend of himself and Greg
- Greg has not responded to reunion
- Jeff writes this book
