# Venue Arcs — Hancock Street, Milledgeville

Five venues. Five arcs. Each unfolds through the chapters as the narrative demands.

**Critical timeline:** In 1989, only two venues existed — the Pool Hall and the Opera House — both owned by DoDo. The strip was BUILT across ~5 years by Jeff's generation.

---

## The Pool Hall / DoDos (C12) — South Side ⭐
**Chapter 5:** The origin. Summer 1989. Jeff's first place in Milledgeville.
**Arc status:** Art generated (2 versions), world-building filled in, chapter draft pending
**Owner:** DoDo (town boss)
**Key character:** Roger — 22yo math/CS student behind the bar. The Oracle.
**Physical:** Wood floors, brick walls, professional snooker tables, antique fixtures. 1950s vibe.
**Crowd:** All men. Gruff locals. Not a college hang.
**Notes:**
The pool hall IS the entry point — to the town, to the strip, to the story. Before everything else.

## The Opera House (C13) — South Side
**Pre-Chapter 1:** The one bar. Thursday nights, everybody goes.
**Arc status:** Named, key facts established
**Owner:** DoDo (same as pool hall)
**Notes:**
In 1989, this was it. The only bar. A girl in Jeff's first 8am class told him: "Thursday nights, the Opera House." The pool hall connects forward to the Opera House arc. DoDo controlled both rooms — the daytime world and the nighttime world.
