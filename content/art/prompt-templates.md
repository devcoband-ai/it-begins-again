# Prompt Templates

Structured templates for generating consistent artwork with AI image tools (Nano Banana, Midjourney, DALL-E, etc.).

---

## Chapter Header Template

```
[Artist Persona]: [The Archivist / The Drifter]
[Scene]: [One-sentence description of the moment]
[Setting]: [Location, time of day, season, year]
[Mood]: [Emotional register — e.g., "quiet recognition," "restless isolation"]
[Palette]: [2-3 dominant colors + accent]
[Texture]: [Film grain / analog / painterly / clean]
[Aspect ratio]: 16:9
[Avoid]: Text, logos, modern technology, anything that breaks period
```

**Example — Chapter 5: The Pool Hall**
```
Artist Persona: The Archivist
Scene: Interior of a small-town pool hall, afternoon light through front windows, brass lamps over snooker tables, an empty lunch counter with a hot dog warmer
Setting: 128 West Hancock Street, Milledgeville, Georgia, summer 1989
Mood: Still. Unhurried. A room that doesn't need you.
Palette: Dark wood brown, green felt, warm brass, dusty amber light
Texture: Heavy film grain, natural light only, analog warmth
Aspect ratio: 16:9
Avoid: People in focus, modern elements, neon, anything clean or renovated
```

## Conceptual Art Template

```
[Artist Persona]: The Conductor
[Concept]: [The geometric or cosmic idea being visualized]
[Structure]: [Pentagon / hexagon / bodygraph / wave interference / fractal]
[Energy]: [Static / kinetic / pulsing / expanding]
[Palette]: [Deep field color + light source color]
[Texture]: [Clean geometric / organic / cosmic]
[Aspect ratio]: 1:1
[Avoid]: Literal representation, human figures, text
```

**Example — The Pattern Resynchronizing**
```
Artist Persona: The Conductor
Concept: Five points of a pentagon dimmed for decades, now brightening — a sixth point appearing at the edge, changing the geometry
Structure: Pentagon transitioning to hexagon, connective lines between all nodes
Energy: Pulsing — the five original nodes glow steady, the sixth flickers
Palette: Deep indigo field, nodes in warm amber, connections in white-gold
Texture: Clean geometric with subtle organic interference at the edges
Aspect ratio: 1:1
Avoid: Literal instruments, faces, band imagery
```

## Album Art Template

```
[Artist Persona]: The Transmitter
[Song title]: [Name]
[Seed origin]: [Which seed this derives from]
[Mood]: [The emotional frequency of the track]
[Visual hook]: [One dominant visual element]
[Palette]: [High contrast — dark base + 1-2 accent colors]
[Aspect ratio]: 1:1 (3000x3000)
[Avoid]: Photographs, busy composition, anything that doesn't read at thumbnail
```

**Example — Rare Radio (Take Me With You)**
```
Artist Persona: The Transmitter
Song title: Take Me With You
Seed origin: Rare Radio — David + Noah instrumental
Mood: Driving. Tender underneath. Burning houses at a safe distance.
Visual hook: A single radio frequency line cutting across a dark field, bending upward at the center like a signal finding its receiver
Palette: Black field, signal line in warm amber, faint blood-red glow at the horizon
Aspect ratio: 1:1 (3000x3000)
Avoid: Literal radios, cars, people, text other than title
```

## Emotional Beat Template

```
[Artist Persona]: The Drifter
[Moment]: [The feeling, not the event]
[Sensory anchor]: [What the viewer should almost feel — heat, stillness, distance]
[Palette]: [Warm and soft — amber, ochre, faded blue]
[Texture]: [Soft focus, light bleed, heat shimmer]
[Aspect ratio]: [16:9 for chapters / 1:1 for standalone]
[Avoid]: Sharp edges, high contrast, anything clinical
```

**Example — The Fifteen-Year Gap**
```
Artist Persona: The Drifter
Moment: The space between. Not loss — just distance. Two frequencies still running, below the threshold, unaware of each other.
Sensory anchor: The feeling of driving past a town you used to live in without stopping
Palette: Faded highway grey, warm amber from a setting sun, blue-dusk sky
Texture: Heavy soft focus, light bleeding at horizons, heat shimmer on asphalt
Aspect ratio: 16:9
Avoid: People, buildings, anything specific — this is about the space between specifics
```
