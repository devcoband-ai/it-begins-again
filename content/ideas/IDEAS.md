# Ideas

A shared space for David, Greg, Rich, Noah, and Jeff.

Drop anything here — a phrase, a sketch, a half-formed thought, a melody you hummed into your phone, a memory, a question. It doesn't need to be finished. It doesn't need to be good. It just needs to exist somewhere other than your head.

Ideas that grow into something will move to `writing/`, `art/`, or `music/` when they're ready. Until then, they live here.
