# Culture Development — Silence

Use this for each distinct culture, subculture, or social stratum in the world.

---

## Culture Name
_What do they call themselves? What do others call them?_

## Relationship to the Vibration
- [ ] Fully immersed (unaware it exists)
- [ ] Immersed but uneasy (senses something wrong)
- [ ] Aware but compliant (knows, doesn't resist)
- [ ] Resistant (actively seeks silence)
- [ ] Outside the system (never entered or escaped)
- [ ] Operates the system (architects, maintainers)

## Beliefs
- What do they believe about reality?
- What do they believe about time? (Short-horizon? Cyclical? Cosmic?)
- What do they believe about the self? (Fixed? Fluid? Constructed? Seed-based?)
- What do they believe silence is? (Peace? Danger? Myth? Medicine?)

## Language
- What words do they have that others don't?
- What words have they lost?
- How do they communicate meaning? (Speech? Visual? Mathematical? Gestural?)
- What can they say that the dominant culture can't express?

## Practices
- Daily rituals or routines?
- How do they gather? (Physical? Virtual? Secret?)
- Do they have arts? What form?
- Do they have a relationship to sacred geometry, sound, or mathematics?

## Social Structure
- How are they organized? (Hierarchy? Network? Fluid?)
- How do they make decisions?
- What is valued? (Knowledge? Stillness? Courage? Craft?)
- What is forbidden or taboo?

## History
- How did this culture form?
- What do they remember that others have forgotten?
- What is their origin story?
- What do they preserve?

## Relationship to Other Cultures
- Who are their allies? Enemies? Unknowns?
- How do they interact with the dominant system?
- What do they trade? (Information? Shelter? Skills? Silence itself?)

## Vulnerabilities
- What could destroy them?
- What internal contradictions do they carry?
- Where are they wrong about themselves?

## Role in the Story
- Why does this culture matter to the narrative?
- What do they provide that no other group can?

## Notes
_Connections, contradictions, open questions._
