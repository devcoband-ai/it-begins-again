# World Building Worksheet — Silence

---

## I. The Surface World

### Power Structure
- Who are the tech monopolies? How many? Do they compete or coordinate?
- What do they sell? (Devices? Services? Experiences? Access?)
- How does the population perceive them? (Beloved? Invisible? Necessary?)
- Is there a government, or have the monopolies absorbed governance?

### Economy
- What do people do for work?
- What is the currency? (Money? Attention? Data? Something new?)
- What does wealth look like? What does poverty look like?
- Is there a class system? How many tiers?

### Technology
- What's the dominant interface? (Wearable? Implanted? Ambient? Neural?)
- Is there an "offline" option? What's the social cost of choosing it?
- What technology from today has been abandoned? What persists?
- What's the most advanced thing most people never think about?

### Daily Life
- What does a normal day look like for an average person?
- What do people do for meaning? (Entertainment? Community? Ritual?)
- What do relationships look like? (Connection-rich but coherence-poor?)
- What do people believe they are free to do?

---

## II. The Control Layer

### Origin
- Was the vibrational system designed or did it emerge?
- If designed: by whom, when, and why?
- If emergent: what were the incentive structures that produced it?
- Does anyone alive know its full architecture?

### Modalities
- **Sound:** How is it transmitted? What frequencies? Constant or pulsed?
- **Linguistics:** How has language been shaped? New words? Lost words? Syntax shifts?
- **Mathematics:** What patterns are people exposed to? Through what channels?
- **Sacred geometry:** Where does it appear? Architecture? Interfaces? Art?
- **Metaphysical idioms:** What do people "believe"? How many belief systems circulate?

### Mechanism of Control
- How does connection-without-coherence work in practice?
- What does it feel like from inside? (Stimulating? Peaceful? Urgent?)
- How does the system respond to someone approaching coherence?
- Is there active suppression, or just overwhelming noise?
- Can the system detect silence in a person?

### Infrastructure
- What is the physical substrate? (Towers? Embedded in materials? Atmospheric?)
- Are there geographic boundaries to coverage?
- What maintains it? Who services it?
- What would it take to disrupt it?

---

## III. The Silence

### What is silence in this world?
- Is it the literal absence of sound, or something deeper?
- Is it a state of mind? A place? A practice? A frequency?
- Can it be taught, or must it be discovered?
- Is it contagious?

### History of Silence
- Was there a time before the vibration?
- Do any records of that time survive?
- Are there oral traditions, myths, or suppressed texts?
- Does anyone remember?

### Geography of Silence
- Are there dead zones where the signal doesn't reach?
- Natural formations that block it? (Mountains, caves, water?)
- Were silent zones mapped and eliminated, or do some persist?
- Can silence be created artificially? (Shielding? Counter-frequency?)

### The Experience
- What happens to someone who achieves silence for the first time?
- What do they perceive that others can't?
- Is it disorienting? Terrifying? Ecstatic?
- What is the cost of returning to the vibration after experiencing silence?
- Can you go back to not-knowing?

## IV. History & Timeline

### Before the Vibration
- What was the world like?
- What event or process initiated the current state?
- Was there a transition period? How long?
- What was lost? What was gained?

### Key Events
- _List the turning points that shaped this world._

### The Present Moment
- Where is the world now?
- Is the system stable, decaying, or intensifying?
- What pressures are building?
- What does the population sense but can't articulate?
