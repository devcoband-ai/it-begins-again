## Sound
- **Ambient:** What's the constant background? (Hum, tone, music, chatter, white noise?)
- **Frequency character:** Low and heavy? High and thin? Layered? Shifting?
- **Source:** Where does it come from? Can you point to it? Or is it everywhere?
- **Absence:** What happens when it stops? Has anyone here heard it stop?
- **What sound is missing?** (Birdsong? Wind? Unaccompanied human voice? True quiet?)

## Sight
- **Light quality:** Natural? Artificial? Filtered? What color temperature?
- **Geometry:** What shapes dominate the environment? (Curves? Grids? Fractals? Spirals?)
- **Screens/surfaces:** What displays information? How much visual data is ambient?
- **Movement:** Is the visual field still or constantly animated?
- **What can't you see?** What's hidden, obscured, or designed to be invisible?

## Touch
- **Surfaces:** What do people touch? (Smooth? Warm? Vibrating? Frictionless?)
- **Air:** Temperature, humidity, pressure — does the air feel alive or dead?
- **Vibration:** Can you feel the frequency in your body? Where? (Chest? Teeth? Skin?)
- **Contact:** How much human-to-human touch exists? Is it common or rare?

## Smell
- **Dominant:** What does this place smell like?
- **Engineered vs. organic:** Are the smells designed? Natural? Mixed?
- **Memory triggers:** Do any smells connect to the world before the vibration?
- **Absence:** What smells have disappeared from the world?

## Taste
- **Food culture:** What do people eat? Is food real, synthetic, optimized?
- **Flavor range:** Has taste been engineered for pleasure? Or has it flattened?
- **The taste of the air:** Does the atmosphere have a taste? Metallic? Sweet? Nothing?

## The Sixth Sense (Proprioception / Interiority)
- **Body awareness:** How connected are people to their own bodies here?
- **Internal state:** What does it feel like *inside* to be in this place? (Buzzing? Numb? Scattered? Alert?)
- **Coherence level:** Can you hold a single thought here? For how long?
- **Silence proximity:** How close is this place to silence? What would it take to get there?
